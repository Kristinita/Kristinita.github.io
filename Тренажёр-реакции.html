The Online Reaction Time Test

Instructions:

  1. Click the large button on the right to begin.
  2. Wait for the stoplight to turn green.
  3. When the stoplight turns green, click the large button quickly!
  4. Click the large button again to continue.

Hints:

  • The stoplight may take up to seven seconds to change. The amount of time is random.
  • You may press any key, instead of clicking the mouse button, if you prefer.
  • You will be tested five times, and your average reaction time will be calculated.

Using the Reaction Time Test offline:

A number of people have e-mailed me to ask how they can use this timer offline. Apparently when one uses the IE browser's Save command, it doesn't collect all the files needed.

So to make this easier, here are links to ALL the files used in the Reaction Time Test, and you can collect them yourself. The Reaction Time Test script expects to find all the images in an '../images/reaction_trainer_images' subfolder, directly below the folder holding the html file.
